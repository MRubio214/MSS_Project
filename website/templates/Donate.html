Steps For Donating

Fill out the form and supplies/clothing you want to donate, gather your stuff, and head to the donation location. There you will be able to drop off your supplies or clothing there.

Steps for donating school supplies:

School supplies must either be new or slightly used and in good condition.

Steps for donating Uniforms:

  • Uniforms must be in good condition which means no holes and rips in the fabric.
  • Uniforms will not have any stains of any state.
  • Uniforms may be new or used depending on the state its in.
  • We will not take any clothing that has noticeable holes or stains.
